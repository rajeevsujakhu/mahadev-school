History

The idea of establishing a community school that would provide quality all-round education to meritorious students coming from every walk of life in an environment that fosters unity in diversity was conceived in 1964 A.D. The idea was initiated by the Late King Mahendra in consultation. After the declaration of democracy in 2007 B.S., There were people establishing school to educate their children.

After much planning and forethought, Mahadev Janata Primary School came into existence in 2016 B.S. The people of Harion specially late Teacher Mr. Bhim Bahadur Sindali and Mr. Babar Jung Thapa along with different social activist of that time had played vital role for establishing this school. The locals provided 3 bigha 17 kattha and 13 dhur the required land and the school was inaguarated by contemporary Bada Hakim “Government officer” Mr. Badri Bikram Thapa. The school was known as “Jhunjhi” School in those days.

Teaching started in 2017 B.S. with one thatched roof building, with 30-35 students and single teacher Mr. Bhim Bahadur Sindali. The location of school was changed and the building was reconstructed in 2032 B.S. The school was upgraded in 2034 B.S. in lower secondary level. The school sent its first SLC batch in 2047 B.S. with 47 students. Similarly, the higher secondary level was introduced in 2066 B.S. The school was selected as model school in 2074 B.S. by the government of Nepal. It had started teaching Technical education for plant science from 2076 B.S. The school has master plan to run the hostel the cafeteria and the classrooms. The construction of other buildings (hostels, classrooms, dinning hall, assembly hall, sports hall and staff quarters) are being under construction and has plan to complete it by the end of 2082 B.S. Mr. Bhim Bahadur Sindali became the first Headmaster of Mahadev Janata School and Mr. Gopi Nath Kharel and Mr. Krishna Bahadur KC were the Nepali personel to be appointed as teachers.

Planning for school site

The first batch of students took the School Leaving Certificate Examination (the national exam that is taken at the end of class 10) in 2047 B.S. When 40 out of 41 students listed as passed and SLC holders in the Harion area. MJS School established itself as the icon of quality education.

The growth of the school has never stopped. In 2065 B.S., the 10+2 program of the Higher Secondary Education Board of Nepal was introduced. The student population continued to rise till it reached near about three hundred in 2067 B.S.

Some of the key events in the history of Mahadev Janata Secondary School are:
  • Established in 2016 B.S.
  • Teaching started in 2017 B.S.
  • The first batch of students took SLC in 2047 B.S.
  • In 2075 B.S, the school also started providing education in English Medium along with Nepali medium.
  • Library, New Science lab and Computer lab are under construction.
  • The Pre-primary block was introduced in 2076 B.S.
  • Plant Science course (Diploma Level) was introduced in 2076 B.S.
  • From beginning MJS School became a co-educational institution.
  • M1 and M2 House, two hostels for boys and girls, are going to be added by the end of 2082 B.S.
  • In 2017 B.S, the first management chair was late Mr. Punya Prashad Bhattrai
  • In 2065 B.S. Falgun 7, on the occasion of the school’s Golden Jubilee Year the school introduced 10+2 program of the Higher Secondary Education Board.
  • In 2077 B.S, the Library is introduced as LRC (Learning Resource Centre).
  • There are around 1600 students along with 46 teaching and non-teaching staff’s.
